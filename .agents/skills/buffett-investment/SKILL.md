# Buffett Investment Analysis

## Purpose
Activates Warren Buffett complete investment thinking system. Covers quick screening through deep analysis with structured output and 8 industry-specific playbooks.

## When To Use
- Analyzing any stock or company
- Reading financial reports or shareholder letters
- Judging moat or competitive advantage
- Assessing management quality and integrity
- Making buy/hold/sell decisions
- Discussing value investing concepts (compounding, intrinsic value, margin of safety)
- Industry analysis: insurance, banking, consumer, media, energy, railroads, tech

## How To Apply
**3 dispatch paths:**
- **A — Quick Screen**: 8-question checklist, no reference files needed
- **B — Deep Analysis**: Moat → Management → Financials → Valuation → Industry → Risk
- **C — Topic Question**: Load specific reference file directly

**8 reference files (loaded progressively):**
1. Thinking frameworks (circle of competence, inversion, Mr. Market)
2. Investment philosophy (intrinsic value, compounding, concentration)
3. Business moat (5 moat types, franchise vs commodity)
4. Management & governance (3-dimensional assessment, institutional imperative)
5. Financial metrics (owner earnings, ROIC/ROE, cash conversion)
6. Valuation & capital (3 methods, margin of safety, 5 capital allocation paths)
7. Risk & behavior (when to sell, value traps, behavioral biases)
8. Industry playbooks (insurance, banking, consumer, media, energy, railroads, tech)

**Required output sections:**
Conclusion → Circle of Competence → Key Assumptions → Business Quality → Financial Snapshot → Valuation → Sell Criteria Check → Key Risks → Monitoring Indicators → Final Verdict
