SECTION 7 — IMAGE CONTAINER STRATEGY (FOR SECTION IMAGES)
================================================================================

For section images and hero image, use this STRONG container pattern:

HERO or SECTION IMAGE:
<div style="position:relative;width:100%;max-width:960px;margin:0 auto;">
  <div style="position:relative;padding-bottom:56.25%;height:0;overflow:hidden;">
    <img src="[IMAGE_URL]" alt="[ALT]" style="position:absolute;top:0;right:0;width:100%;height:100%;object-fit:cover;border-radius:8px;" loading="lazy">
  </div>
</div>

This creates a 16:9 aspect ratio container that WordPress cannot break.

ALTERNATIVE (simpler, for product-like images):
<div style="width:100%;height:auto;border-radius:8px;margin:1.5rem 0;display:block;">
  <img src="[IMAGE_URL]" alt="[ALT]" style="width:100%;height:auto;border-radius:8px;" loading="lazy">
</div>
