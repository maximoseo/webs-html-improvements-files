Frequently Asked

Answers for IT leadership.

How much can we realistically save?

Customers typically reduce PC energy consumption by up to 60%. Full ROI — including software, deployment, and services — is achieved in as little as under four months.

Does this scale to our environment?

PowerPlug Pro is deployed across fleets ranging from 100 PCs to more than 50,000, including Clalit Health Services with tens of thousands of endpoints.

Will it disrupt our users or patching cycles?

No. Policies are behavior-aware and integrate with SCCM and AD. The Wake-on-LAN Mesh ensures PCs are reliably available for patching, updates, and backup windows.

What does deployment look like?

Setup is intuitive and client distribution leverages your existing tools. Most environments are fully operational within hours, with no network reconfiguration required.

Is there a security benefit?

Yes. Powering down and disconnecting idle endpoints materially reduces the attack surface, complementing existing EDR, patching, and zero-trust strategies.
