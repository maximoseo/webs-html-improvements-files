How long does deployment take?

Deployment is exceptionally fast. Thanks to intuitive setup and client distribution through tools like SCCM, you can have PowerPlug Pro up and running across your network in a matter of hours.

No. Our Patented Wake-on-LAN Mesh technology traverses multiple subnets and complex networks without requiring any changes to your existing network switches or routers.

What is the typical ROI?

Organizations typically see a full return on investment (payback) in as low as under 4 months, driven by up to 60% savings on PC energy use.

The Wake Up Portal allows end users and IT technicians to securely wake their allowed computers remotely, ensuring zero disruption to productivity.
