## 33 HARD RULES (each rule below is numbered; map each Acceptance Criterion back to its rule)

### Rule 1 — Real social links
The About the Author section MUST contain at least three real powerplug.ai company social account links. Use only the real accounts listed above. No `#` placeholders. No invented accounts.

### Rule 2 — Logo links to homepage
The logo in the About the Author section MUST be wrapped in `<a href="https://powerplug.ai/">` with `aria-label` set.

### Rule 3 — Author section quality
The About the Author section MUST look professional: real logo image, real company name or editorial-team byline, brand colors, clean spacing, socials arranged consistently. It MUST render cleanly on 320 px, 768 px, and 1440 px viewports.

### Rule 4 — No numeric prefixes in TOC
TOC heading texts MUST NOT start with numeric prefixes. The regex `^\d+[\.\)]\s*` must match zero TOC link texts.

### Rule 5 — TOC placement
The TOC MUST be placed in the DOM immediately after the first `<p>` OR first `<img>` inside the article body. Never as the first child. Never later than the first content element.

### Rule 6 — TOC + FAQ collapsed by default
TOC and FAQ MUST start collapsed. Implementation: `<details>` elements WITHOUT the `open` attribute. Must toggle correctly in standalone HTML and inside WordPress Gutenberg Custom HTML block. Chevron icon must rotate ≥90° on open with CSS `transition: transform 0.3s ease`.

### Rule 7 — TOC + FAQ consistent with brand
TOC and FAQ MUST share identical border-radius, padding, font-size tokens (±2 px tolerance), icon style, and hover behavior. Colors MUST be drawn from the brand palette above.

### Rule 8 — Mid-article CTAs
Insert at least two contextually relevant mid-article CTA blocks between major content sections. Each CTA block: headline + 1–2 sentences of copy + one branded button. The button `href` MUST be `https://powerplug.ai/contact-us`.

### Rule 9 — End-of-article CTA
Insert one stronger end-of-article CTA block immediately before the FAQ. The button must be ≥20% larger than the mid-article CTA buttons. Background uses the brand primary purple gradient. At least two lines of persuasive copy.

### Rule 10 — Contact conversion in ≥3 places
The article MUST contain contact-focused conversion elements in at least three total locations. Every CTA `href` MUST resolve to `https://powerplug.ai/contact-us` or be a `tel:` / `mailto:` link using the real phone/email above. No `#`. No `javascript:void(0)`.

### Rule 11 — Floating Contact button
Add a fixed-position floating Contact button: bottom-right on LTR / bottom-left on RTL. Minimum 44×44 px tap target. Real contact URL. Hover state with color shift + lift. Minimum 8 px gap from any other fixed element. Use `env(safe-area-inset-bottom)` for safe-area support.

### Rule 12 — Floating Scroll-to-Top
Add a floating Scroll-to-Top button that is hidden until `window.scrollY > 300`. Animated appearance via opacity + transform over ≥200 ms. Click triggers `window.scrollTo({top:0, behavior:'smooth'})`. Hover state. Minimum 8 px gap from Contact button. Stays within viewport on ≤480 px screens.

### Rule 13 — Enrichment blocks
Weave at least three distinct enrichment blocks naturally into the body — one Tip, one How-To, one Did You Know. Each has a distinct icon, tinted background from the palette, and colored left-border accent.

### Rule 14 — Exactly one H1
The final article MUST contain exactly one `<h1>`. Any subsequent prominent text MUST use `<h2>`, `<h3>`, etc. Hierarchy must be strict: H1 > H2 > H3.

### Rule 15 — Internal + external links
Include at least three internal links to `powerplug.ai/*` pages and at least two external links to authoritative third-party domains (e.g. energystar.gov, iea.org, EU/US regulators, major academic or industry sources). External links use `target="_blank"` and `rel="noopener"`. All hrefs must be real and verifiable.

### Rule 16 — Full content + real images
Article body ≥1,500 words. At least three real images with valid `src` URLs, descriptive `alt` text, `loading="lazy"`, and defined aspect-ratio or width/height. No lorem ipsum. No empty paragraphs.

### Rule 17 — Real content structure
Use semantic `<section>` blocks. Each section ≥1 heading + ≥1 paragraph of ≥40 words. At least six semantic sections total (e.g. intro, problem, solution, implementation, measurement, FAQ, conclusion).

### Rule 18 — No dates in intro/hero
The first paragraph, the intro section, and the hero area MUST contain zero years, zero months, zero date references, and zero relative time references. Regex `\b(19|20)\d{2}\b` must match zero times in the first 200 words of body copy. Dates are permitted later only where contextually required.

### Rule 19 — WordPress-safe + responsive
Inline `<style>` in `<head>` only. Zero external stylesheet links. Zero external JS CDN dependencies. No jQuery. Must render with no horizontal scroll at 320 px, 375 px, 768 px, 1024 px, and 1440 px. Body max-width 680–780 px.

### Rule 20 — Gutenberg safe
Output must paste cleanly into a Gutenberg Custom HTML block and render identically. JS encapsulated in an IIFE.

### Rule 21 — Floating buttons never overlap content on mobile
At 375 px viewport, floating buttons must not overlap any CTA, heading, paragraph, or image.

### Rule 22 — Author, social, CTA, FAQ, TOC, content blocks all responsive
Visual QA at 375/768/1024/1440 px must show every component rendering correctly — no wrapped text overlap, no cut-off buttons, no cramped spacing.

### Rule 23 — Correct logo + brand colors
Logo `src` must be the real live logo URL above. At least three brand hex values from the palette must appear in computed styles.

### Rule 24 — Real trust signals
Include at least one real trust signal: real customer quote with attribution, OR real business detail (address, phone, registration) sourced from the live site. Never fabricate. If unavailable, state "Based on publicly available information" in the validation note.

### Rule 25 — Hover effects
Every CTA button, TOC item, FAQ item, and social button MUST have a CSS `:hover` state. Social hover uses the official network color:
- LinkedIn #0A66C2
- Facebook #1877F2
- X / Twitter #000000
- YouTube #FF0000
- Instagram #E4405F
Focus states must be visible for keyboard navigation. All text must meet WCAG AA contrast (≥4.5:1 normal, ≥3:1 large).

### Rule 26 — Readability
Body max-width 680–780 px. Line-height ≥1.6. H1 > H2 > H3 > p size steps of ≥4 px. Section spacing ≥24 px, ≤80 px.

### Rule 27 — Consistent alignment
All icon+text pairs use `display:inline-flex; align-items:center; gap:6–8px`. All buttons share consistent border-radius, padding, and alignment tokens.

### Rule 28 — No low-contrast states
Default, hover, focus, active, and visited states for all text, buttons, links, and icons must pass WCAG AA against their background.

### Rule 29 — Working images
Every `<img>` must return HTTP 200, have non-empty descriptive `alt` text, and use `object-fit: cover` or `contain` with a defined aspect ratio.

### Rule 30 — Premium look
Overall layout must feel premium and brand-specific, not generic or obviously AI-generated. Use ≤2 font families. Use a consistent 4/8 px spacing scale.

### Rule 31 — Section balance
No section vertical spacing less than 24 px or greater than 80 px. No oversized gaps. No cramped sections.

### Rule 32 — Accessible link/button states
All link and button states remain legible and readable. Keyboard focus indicators are visible.

### Rule 33 — RTL / Hebrew support
When the body has `dir="rtl"`:
- TOC and FAQ chevron icons appear AFTER the heading text on the same line at a fixed consistent position.
- CTA buttons and floating buttons with icon+text place the icon after the text.
- Hover color changes never drop contrast below 4.5:1.
